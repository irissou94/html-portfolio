About Me

🌿 Newbie yoga teacher with a 500YTT certification, passionate about sharing the transformative power of mindfulness. I believe in the art of letting go of the past while cherishing the lessons it brings—each experience shapes our future.

✨ When I'm not on the mat, you’ll find me exploring new flavors in the kitchen or traveling to discover the beauty of different cultures. Join me on this journey of growth, connection, and delicious living!

🧘‍♀️ Yoga is more than a practice for me; it’s a way of life. I strive to create a safe space where students can cultivate self-awareness, embrace their journeys, and foster a deeper connection to themselves and others. Each class is an opportunity to explore the interplay between movement, breath, and mindfulness.

🌍 Travel fuels my soul, allowing me to connect with diverse communities and find inspiration in their stories. Whether I’m cooking a dish inspired by my travels or unwinding in a new place, I believe that every experience adds color to our lives. Let’s embark on this adventure together, nourishing our bodies, minds, and spirits!
